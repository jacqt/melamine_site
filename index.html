About Yass

Yass is like a coffee shop where you can meet guys in the community. We're not just a dating ap. We're not a hookup app. We're just a place that's designed for gay guys to hangout, meet new people, and if you feel like it, coordinate to explore the city. But of course - do what feels right ;).

Things to know
  1. Everyone's friendly and approachable.
  2. Don't be shy - just say hi.
  3. Welcome to San Francisco!
