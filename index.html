About Yass

Yass is like a coffee shop where you can meet guys in the community. We're not just a dating app. We're not a hookup app. We're just a place that's designed for gay guys to hangout, meet new people, and if you feel like it, coordinate to explore the city. But of course - do what feels right ;).

Things to know
1. Everyone's friendly and approachable.
2. Don't be shy - just say hi.
3. Welcome to San Francisco!

Help!

Got a question? Hopefully we'll have an answer! Please send your questions to helpme@yassplease.com

Jobs

At Yass you'll be tackling challenges and solving problems you can intimately relate to. Our hope is to build the strongest team to help connect folks in the community and make them feel welcome no matter where they are.

It's a tough road. No doubt. But anything's possible with a badass team, some luck, and the ability to envision yourself succeed. We hope you'll join us!
